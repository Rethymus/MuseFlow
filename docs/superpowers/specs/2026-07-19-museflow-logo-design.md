# MuseFlow Logo Design

## Context

MuseFlow 灵韵 is positioned as an author-led long-form writing tool: the product helps writers capture fragments, organize structure, guard promises, and polish prose without replacing the author's control.

The logo should therefore avoid a generic AI-software mark. It needs to communicate literary creation, long-form structure, and careful craft: a tool that turns scattered imagination into a coherent story while preserving human warmth.

## Approved Direction

The approved direction is **结构星图**.

A long-form novel is represented as a structure star map. Nodes represent **章节**, **角色**, **伏笔**, and **世界规则**. Lines represent **因果**, **承诺**, **回收**, and **故事弧**. The overall silhouette may read as an abstract open book, a wing, or **M 形负空间**.

The mark should feel like modern technology supporting story architecture, not a generic AI badge. The central metaphor is not "machine writes text"; it is "the author sees and stewards the living structure of a novel."

## Visual Language

- **Primary form:** connected star-map nodes arranged to imply a book spread, wing, or MuseFlow "M" without literal illustration.
- **Node hierarchy:** a few larger anchor nodes can imply major chapters or arcs; smaller nodes can imply characters, clues, rules, and local beats.
- **Line behavior:** lines should feel intentional and authored, showing causality and payoff rather than random network noise.
- **Mood:** precise, quiet, literary, and durable. Avoid flashy sci-fi effects that would make the brand feel like a stock AI product.
- **Color direction:** deep ink or midnight blue background; warm gold or jade-white structure lines; subtle cyan-blue accent for a small technological glint.

## Negative Constraints

Do not use any of the following motifs or presentation styles:

- robot
- chip
- brain
- circuit
- chat bubble
- AI letters
- stock icon
- watermark
- fake UI
- screenshot

These constraints are part of the brand direction. The logo should not imply automated writing, chatbot output, or generic machine intelligence.
